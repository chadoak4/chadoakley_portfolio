Who am I?

Highly motivated business intelligence professional. Successfully demonstrated leadership abilities in the healthcare industry leading a hospital's business department to new heights in areas of collections, revenue, and higher standards of quality service for our patients.

Now more than ever it is important to understand technology. We live in a society that is continually driven and evolving through the use of technology advancements. Understanding this, I recently took leave from a position in the business world to dive feet first into the world of technology. I expanded my education and took a grueling three-month eighty-hour week course on web development that included topics on databases, social media, marketing, Ruby on Rails, and front-end frameworks. Since graduating from this course I have proven my skills in a local start-up company developing and maintaining a CRM, ERP in-house application with Ruby on Rails, JS component based framework Vue.Js, SQL, HTML, and CSS.
